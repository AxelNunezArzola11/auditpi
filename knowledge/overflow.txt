INTEGER OVERFLOW AND UNDERFLOW (SWC-101)
Severity: HIGH

Solidity below 0.8.0 arithmetic wraps silently.

VULNERABLE:
uint256 balance = 0;
balance -= 1; // underflow: becomes 2^256-1

FIXED:
Use Solidity 0.8.0 or higher (built-in checks).
Or use SafeMath: balance = balance.sub(amount);

Real exploit: BEC Token 2018, unlimited tokens minted.
Keywords: uint, int, SafeMath, unchecked, pragma solidity ^0.7, ^0.6
